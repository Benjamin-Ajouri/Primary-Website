About

YN

Hi — I'm Benjamin Ajouri, a designer working at the intersection of UI/UX, graphic design, and illustration. I believe the best design feels inevitable: like it could never have been any other way.

I studied at Winona State University and have since spent over 3 years working in Idesign. I'm drawn to work that has a distinct point of view — design that takes a position.

When I'm not designing, I'm drawing, reading about architecture, playing minecraft, or trying to find the perfect spot for relaxation.
